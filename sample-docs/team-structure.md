## Communication Norms

### Slack Channels
- `#engineering` — General engineering discussion
- `#frontend` / `#backend` / `#platform-team` — Team-specific
- `#code-review` — PR review requests (post with link)
- `#deploys` — Deployment notifications
- `#incidents` — Active incidents (use @here sparingly)
- `#random` — Fun stuff, memes, watercooler
- `#introductions` — Introduce yourself when you join!

### Meeting Schedule
| Meeting | When | Duration | Who |
|---------|------|----------|-----|
| Daily standup | 10:00 AM | 15 min | Your team |
| Sprint planning | Mon every 2 weeks | 1 hour | Your team + PM |
| Tech sync | Thursday 3 PM | 45 min | All engineering |
| All-hands | First Friday of month | 1 hour | Everyone |
| 1:1 with manager | Weekly | 30 min | You + manager |

### Async-First Culture
- Default to Slack messages, Linear comments, or Loom videos
- Only schedule meetings when async won't work
- Core overlap hours: 10 AM – 2 PM EST
- Outside core hours, don't expect immediate responses
- Use threads in Slack (never reply in channel for discussions)
